Common Questions

FAQ

Do you do hair AND makeup?

+

Yes! That's one of the things that makes Bridal Glam by Kaitlyn unique. You get both hair and makeup from one artist, meaning your full look is cohesive and you don't need to coordinate between multiple stylists.

How far in advance should I book?

+

For weddings, I recommend reaching out as early as possible — 6 to 12 months in advance is ideal, especially for peak spring and fall dates. Prom and other events can usually be booked with less lead time, but sooner is always better!

Do you offer bridal trials?

+

Yes, and I highly recommend them! A trial session lets us test out your look before the big day so there are no surprises. It's also a great chance to adjust and perfect everything so you feel 100% confident.

Where are you located / do you travel?

+

I'm based in Northern Colorado and serve Loveland, Fort Collins, Greeley, Kersey, and surrounding areas. Travel availability and fees may apply depending on your location — just ask!

How much do services cost?

+

Pricing varies based on the services needed, number of people, and travel. I offer custom quotes so you get accurate pricing for your specific event. Reach out and I'll put together something tailored to you!

Do you work with all skin types and tones?

+

Absolutely. Beauty has no one look — I work with all skin tones, textures, and types. My kit is stocked to create beautiful looks for everyone.
